# Marcellus Trip

Starting location: Marcellus, NY, USA

This seed itinerary covers a 12-day RV trip starting April 13, 2026 from Marcellus, New York. The route heads west through Rochester to Cambridge, OH, then south through Cumberland Falls, KY and Grimsley, TN with a Cathedral Caverns State Park midpoint before a multi-night Birmingham stay. From there it moves to the Gulf Coast, Pensacola Beach, and Atlanta before returning north through Roanoke, VA and Niagara back to Marcellus on April 24.

## Route Overview

Marcellus, NY -> Rochester, NY -> Cambridge, OH -> Cumberland Falls, KY -> Grimsley, TN -> Cathedral Caverns State Park, AL -> Birmingham, AL -> Gulf State Park Campground, AL -> Pensacola Beach, FL -> Atlanta, GA -> Roanoke, VA -> Buffalo / Niagara, NY -> Marcellus, NY
